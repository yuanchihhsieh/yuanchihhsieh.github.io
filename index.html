About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Applied Mathematics, Statistics, and Scientific Computation (AMSC) program at the University of Maryland, College Park. I hold double B.S. degrees in Computer Science and in Meteorology and Atmospheric Science from the Pennsylvania State University.

I am currently advised by Professor Kayo Ide. My research focuses on using numerical methods to solve physical problems, with interests in:

  • Numerical weather prediction
  • Numerical analysis
  • Machine learning
